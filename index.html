№ 03

Present Tense

The most interesting problem in finance isn't an accounting problem — it's an organizational one. What does a finance department look like when almost everything it does today no longer needs a person?

At Blazesoft I'm answering that in production, not in a slide deck: AI agents deployed across live finance operations — payment-run reviews, exception monitoring over the group's bank activity, recurring reporting that drafts itself, and an accounting-records database the whole team queries in plain English.

The goal isn't fewer people for its own sake. It's a finance function where humans keep the judgment and machines take everything else — currently tracking to a 25%+ reduction in finance payroll, with a faster close than the team has ever run.

  • ReviewsAutomated payment-run and payroll reviews with exception flags, not spot checks
  • MonitoringContinuous exception monitoring across multi-entity, multi-currency bank activity
  • ReportingRecurring management reporting and reconciliations that draft themselves
  • RecordsAn AI-queryable accounting database — questions in English, answers from the GL
